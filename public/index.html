Rules

Every player is given the same meme with the same topic (unless there is no topic).

Players create a caption to go along with the meme and topic.

Players vote for the best meme.


How to caption a meme:

Drag to reposition and resize the meme.

Click on 'Add Text' to add a text box. This textbox can be repositioned and resized.

Click on 'Reset' to restart captioning the meme.

Click on 'Submit' to finalize and submit your meme.

Selected objects automatically are brought up to the first layer. To deselect an object and reset its layer, simply click outside of the meme box.
